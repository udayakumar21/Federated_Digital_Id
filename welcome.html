About Us

FDI is a platform for the access,storage and sharing of individual identity documents.

The federated digital identity allows individuals to use a single digital identity across multiple platforms and applications, without the need for multiple IDs or sharing personal information with each department. This enables individuals to maintain control over their personal information and privacy while accessing the services they need.
